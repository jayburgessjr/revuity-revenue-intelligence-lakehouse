# Revenue System Map

This is a high-level map of the revenue system that the lakehouse supports.

## Sources

- **CRM**: Leads, accounts, opportunities, reps, activities.
- **Billing / Payments**: Invoices, subscriptions, payments, refunds.
- **Marketing**: Campaigns, ad spend, impressions, clicks, UTM data.

## Stages

1. Visitor
2. Lead
3. MQL (Marketing Qualified Lead)
4. SQL (Sales Qualified Lead)
5. Opportunity
6. Closed Won / Closed Lost
7. Onboarding
8. Active Customer
9. Churned / Inactive

## Data Domains

- **Pipeline**: Leads, accounts, opportunities, stages.
- **Revenue**: Invoices, payments, MRR/ARR.
- **Engagement**: Events, sessions, email activity, ad interactions.

## Outputs

- Funnel conversion dashboards.
- MRR / ARR and cohort views.
- Channel ROI and CAC reporting.
- Alerts and insights for RevBoard.
