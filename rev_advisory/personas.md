# Revenue Personas

These personas describe how different roles use the revenue intelligence lakehouse.

## Founder / CEO

- Cares about: runway, ARR, burn multiple, growth rate, risk.
- Questions:
  - Are we on pace to hit our revenue goals?
  - Which segments and channels are truly profitable?
  - Where are we leaking revenue in the funnel?

## RevOps / Operations Lead

- Cares about: process bottlenecks, SLA breaches, funnel conversion.
- Questions:
  - Where are leads getting stuck or dropped?
  - Which reps, segments, or stages need support?
  - How clean and reliable is our data?

## Marketing Lead

- Cares about: channel performance, CAC, ROAS, attribution.
- Questions:
  - Which campaigns drive the highest-quality opportunities?
  - Where is CAC creeping up?
  - How should we reallocate budget?

## Finance Lead

- Cares about: collections, churn, cash flow, predictability.
- Questions:
  - Are invoices being paid on time?
  - What churn risk is emerging by cohort or segment?
  - How predictable is next quarter's cash?
