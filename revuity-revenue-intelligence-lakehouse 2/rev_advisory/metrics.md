# Revenue Metrics

This document defines the core metrics Revuity Analytics uses in its revenue architecture.

## North Star Metrics

- **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**: Sum of recurring revenue from active subscriptions in the current month.
- **ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)**: 12 × MRR.
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: (Starting MRR + Expansion - Contraction - Churn) / Starting MRR.
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Average revenue per account × average customer lifetime (in months or years).
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Total sales + marketing spend / number of new customers in the period.

## Funnel Metrics

- **Lead → MQL** conversion rate
- **MQL → SQL** conversion rate
- **SQL → Opportunity** conversion rate
- **Opportunity → Closed Won** conversion rate

## Channel Metrics

- CAC by channel (paid search, paid social, referral, direct, organic).
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) by channel.
- Payback period by channel.
